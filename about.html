Host & Founder

NITIN VERMA

Nitin Verma started film production services in 2008, culminating in the foundation of 'CineMartinMedia' in 2010. He and the company went on to execute 100 + advertising films across digital and television platforms, as well as Indian and international feature films. Nitin is driven towards storytelling that touches the soul, regardless of scale.

A humanitarian at heart, his vision is of a world that operates correctly "in the eyes of a child." If a child can question how the world around him/her operates, we are falling short. Nitin personifies this idealistic world view, and works tirelessly towards the micro and macro goals this entails - until a practical reality becomes practically attainable.
